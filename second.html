Hardik history

Hardik Pandya (born 11 October 1993) is an Indian cricketer who is the current vice-captain of the Indian cricket team in limited overs format. A batting all-rounder who can also bowl right-arm fast-medium deliveries, Pandya has represented India in all 3 formats. He is considered to be of the best all-rounders in world cricket. He currently plays for the Baroda cricket team in domestic cricket and the Mumbai Indians the Indian Premier League (IPL).His elder brother Krunal Pandya is also a professional cricketer.
